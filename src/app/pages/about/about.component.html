Our Story

Incorporated in 2013 in India in collaboration with a German Accredited Certification Body for ISO Management System Certifications. The brand evolved to Accric to drive excellence in Governance, Risk, Compliance, and Sustainable Business Solutions. With 11 years of expertise, we help organizations accelerate growth through innovative risk and governance strategies. In 2023, we expanded to the United States and United Arab Emirates, reinforcing our global impact. Our mission is to empower businesses with strategic insights, ensuring compliance and resilience in a rapidly evolving regulatory landscape. Accric is dedicated to delivering confidence, clarity, and accelerated growth through expert solutions.
